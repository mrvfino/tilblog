Day 12: The Clever YouTube "WATC" Exploit

During the infancy of YouTube, a clever internet hoax was made and spread by exploiting the channel redirect link feature of YouTube. Removing the h from the www.youtube.com/watch?v={VIDEO_ID} will cause the rest of the query param to be ignored, in turn causing the link to become a channel link instead of a video link.
